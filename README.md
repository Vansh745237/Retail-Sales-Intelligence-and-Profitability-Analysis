# Retail Sales Intelligence & Profitability Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing retail sales data to uncover valuable business insights and profitability trends. Using Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) and Business Intelligence techniques, the project identifies high-performing regions, profitable product categories, and the impact of discounts on profitability.

The objective is to support data-driven decision-making by transforming raw retail data into actionable business insights.

---

## Dataset Information

The dataset contains retail sales records with the following features:

- Ship Mode
- Segment
- Country
- City
- State
- Postal Code
- Region
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Sales
- Quantity
- Discount
- Profit

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze overall sales and profitability.
- Identify top-performing regions and states.
- Evaluate category-wise sales and profit.
- Measure the impact of discounts on profitability.
- Discover patterns through data visualization.
- Generate business recommendations based on findings.

## Key Findings

- West region generated the highest sales and profit.
- East region emerged as the second strongest market.
- Sales and Profit showed a moderate positive correlation.
- Discounts negatively impacted profitability.
- Certain categories contributed significantly more revenue than others.
- Top-performing states drove a large share of overall sales.

---

## Business Recommendations

1. Increase investment in high-performing regions.
2. Optimize discount strategies to protect profit margins.
3. Focus marketing efforts on top-performing categories.
4. Strengthen operations in low-profit regions.
5. Use data-driven decision-making for future growth.

## Conclusion

This project demonstrates how data analytics can transform retail data into meaningful business insights. Through visualization, profitability analysis, and performance evaluation, organizations can identify growth opportunities and improve strategic decision-making.
